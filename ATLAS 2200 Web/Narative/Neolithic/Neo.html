Neolithic

Here have some clothes to keep you warm!!! NOT! Clothes are considered technology and you are supposed to live without technology! A House or tent? Please! You are sleeping in the cold dark outdoors tonight for the rest of your life! Hope you have someone to snuggle with… On the bright side, when you get up in the morning you can use your hands to dig some holes for crops. Now you can give your hands a rest because we’re going to leg it to whenever we need to travel since sadly the wheel and riding a horse is seriously not a thing.
