20th Century

Life could be so much fun and so easy if it weren’t for the fact that the good technology doesn’t exist no more. Such basic appliances as washing machines, clothes dryers, furnaces, exercise machines, refrigerators, Freezers, electric stoves, and vacuum cleaners. So now you must board wash and hang your clothes, while chopping up fire wood for house warms and salting your food so it won’t go bad. So basically you are in an age of work and effort for life… Also it is a great age to work on your sea sickness and horseback riding cause no cars and no planes so if you want to get anywhere you need a horse and a ship unless you are down to walk and swim for extended periods of time.
